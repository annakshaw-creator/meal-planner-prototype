Roasted Tomato Soup
Prep Time
45 min
Servings
4 People
Ingredients 6 items
2 lbs Vine-ripened tomatoes
6 cloves Garlic, peeled
2 tbsp Olive oil
1 yellow onion, quartered
Fresh basil, handful
2 cups vegetable broth
Instructions
1
Preheat oven to 400°F. Cut tomatoes in half and place on a baking sheet with garlic and onion. Drizzle with olive oil.
2
Roast for 45 minutes until charred and caramelized. Transfer to a blender with fresh basil and broth. Blend until smooth.
3
Season with salt and pepper. Serve with crusty bread or a swirl of cream.
